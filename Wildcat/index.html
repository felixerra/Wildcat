Wildcat Lending Library

A Community Queer Library

Wildcat Lending Library is 190+ titles and counting. We'd love for you to check out the collection; it's yours!

How to Pick-Up

  1. Go to the catalog or check out the selection at Gray Owl Coffeeshop in Norman. (223 E. Gray St)
  2. Choose from available books.
  3. Fill out this form or email us.
  4. We will reply to you to coordinate pick-up or mailing.
    (If you're at Gray Owl, you can walk off with them now!)

How to Drop-Off

  • Easiest way is to leave the book on the LGBTQ+ section of the bookshelves at Gray Owl Coffeeshop in Norman!
  • If you'd like to meet to hand the book off, send email to coordinate time, date, and public location.
  • If returning by mail, send in bubble mailer or box to:
    129 W. Gray St #66, Norman, OK 73070.
